About Us

Thich 3 Nguoi Squad consists of Truong, Vy, Giang and Huy, we specialise in solve problems about Karnaugh Map and Truth Table in subject Discrete math for student who are study Information Technology and learn Discrete math. As a student ourselves, we know what it takes difficult to get you understand this subject successful. Since 10/2020, we have been refining our skills in reducing the function with Karnaugh Map or use Truth Table. We like to keep on top of current solve problems to ensure all result we show are up-to-date and definitely true. We also like to build strong, on-going, working relationships with our clients and like to think that we can be the only web solve Discrete math you will ever need to work with again. Not only are we a friendly and approachable squad, we are also passionate about what we do. So if you have a project and are interested in working with us, do not hesitate to get in touch, we would love to hear from you.
